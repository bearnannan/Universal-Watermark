# Privacy Policy for Universal Watermark

**Last updated:** July 2, 2026

Universal Watermark ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through this Privacy Policy. This policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and how it is safeguarded when you use our Android application (the "App").

## 1. Information We Collect

Universal Watermark is a local utility application. We **do not** collect, store, or transmit your personal data, images, or any other sensitive information to our servers or any third-party services. All data processing happens entirely on your device.

To function properly, the App requires certain permissions on your device:

*   **Camera Permission:** Required to capture photos directly within the app for watermarking.
*   **Location Permission (GPS):** Required **only** if you choose to enable the GPS or Address watermark feature. We use this data strictly to imprint your current location onto the photo. This data is never tracked, logged, or sent to any external server.
*   **Storage/Media Permission:** Required to save the watermarked photos to your device's gallery or file system. We also require permission to read images if you choose to import existing photos for watermarking.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

Any data accessed by the App is used exclusively for the purpose of generating and applying the watermark to your photos locally on your device. 
*   **Your photos** never leave your device unless you manually choose to share them using other apps.
*   **Your location** is only retrieved at the exact moment you take a photo (if the GPS feature is enabled) and is immediately drawn onto the image.

## 3. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics, or advertising networks that collect your personal data. 

## 4. Data Security

Because we do not collect or transmit your data over the internet, your information remains as secure as the device you use to run the App. We recommend using standard device security measures (like a passcode or biometric lock) to protect the data stored on your phone.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

## 6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
